Value 4 Value is a new system for paying podcasters in Bitcoin for their content by the listening minute, over the Lightning Network. See the Podcast Index for more info.

I am not a crypto-enthusiast, and so tend to think in dollars, which feel more "real" to me than blockchain coinage. This page pulls the current value of BTC in USD, and determines how many SAT (100,000,000 SAT = 1 BTC) per minute equals 25¢ per hour of podcast listenting, which is the rate I currently find fair and affordable.
